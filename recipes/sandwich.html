Sandwich

Description

Need a quick lunch? A pick-me-up after a stressful day? Make yourself a sandwhich, leave all those problems behind you by working on this quick meal!

Sandwhiches have been a staple for who-knows-how-long, they can be seen and enjoyed in the park, in your car, as your traveling. You don't even need utensils, it's all there in the grasp of your hands!

Ingredients

  • Bread of choiece
  • Cheese, pick your favorite!
  • Protein, maybe turkey or some chicken breast? Maybe ham!
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato (gotta get our fruit in there!)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Set your bread on an easy-to-clean surface (a plate, or a napkin works!)
  2. Put lettuce on your bread
  3. Cheese on lettuce
  4. Protein on cheese
  5. Reverse the order!
  6. Enjoy!

Conclusion

We've now learned how to make a quick sandwich, it's a very fast meal to get going with hopefully you already have the ingredients and don't have to go out for them.
